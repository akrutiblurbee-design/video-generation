Rhyme title: Studio rhyme
Rhyme:
Ek chhota sa beej maine boya,
Dharti maa ki god mein soya.
Suraj ki kiran ne aakar jagaya,
Barish ki boondon ne pani pilaya.
Dheere dheere nikla ek hara patta,
Badhne laga phir woh paudha achha.
Phool khile hain sundar pyare,
Khushiyan baantein saare jag saare.
Milan hai ye kudrat ka pyaara.
