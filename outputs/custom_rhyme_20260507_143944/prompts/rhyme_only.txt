Title: The Happy Number Train

=== RHYME ===
One little engine chugging down the track,
Two shiny carriages following the back.
Three friendly bunnies hop along the way,
Four yellow ducklings come outside to play.
Five bright balloons floating in the sky,
Six happy birds go soaring way up high.
Seven golden stars twinkle in the light,
Eight sleepy puppies say a soft goodnight,
Nine counting friends make everything feel right.

=== RHYME STYLE ===
AABBCCDDD, rhythmic and whimsical, ages 3-7

=== RHYME MUSIC STYLE PROMPT ===
A gentle, slow-building 60-second children's nursery track starting with a soft, melodic glockenspiel and light acoustic guitar, gradually adding a cheerful, bouncy marimba rhythm and light flute, upbeat and magical tempo, no heavy percussion, bright and clean production.
