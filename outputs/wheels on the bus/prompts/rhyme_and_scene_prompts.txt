Title: The Playful Train

=== RHYME ===
The playful train goes choo-choo-choo,  
Chugging along with a happy crew.  
The engine puffs and the whistle blows,  
Round and round the meadow it goes.  
The cars are bright, red, blue, and green,  
All of them dancing, oh what a scene!  
The friendly animals wave hello,  
As the train rolls by, nice and slow.  
With each soft chug, the fun expands,  
The playful train travels through the lands.  
Hooray for fun, let’s ride today.
