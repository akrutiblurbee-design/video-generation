Title: Chhota Sa Paudha (The Little Plant)

=== RHYME ===
Ek chhota sa beej maine boya,
Dharti maa ki god mein soya.
Suraj ki kiran ne aakar jagaya,
Barish ki boondon ne pani pilaya.
Dheere dheere nikla ek hara patta,
Badhne laga phir woh paudha achha.
Phool khile hain sundar pyare,
Khushiyan baantein saare jag saare.
Milan hai ye kudrat ka pyaara.

=== RHYME STYLE ===
Simple AABB/AABB rhyme scheme with a rhythmic, educational tone focused on nature, ages 3-7.

=== RHYME MUSIC STYLE PROMPT ===
Gentle acoustic guitar and soft xylophone melody, starting slow and light to match a morning garden scene, building into a happy, mid-tempo 1-minute instrumental with playful wind chimes and a cheerful, rhythmic beat suitable for 3D animation.
